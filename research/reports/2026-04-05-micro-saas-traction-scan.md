## 1. AI Wrappers / Tools Built on LLM APIs — The Dominant Category

The highest-revenue micro-SaaS products right now are "GPT wrappers" built on Claude/OpenAI APIs. The market is large but winners have already emerged in most categories.

### Products with confirmed traction:

| Product | What it does | Revenue | Pricing | Gap it leaves |
|---------|-------------|---------|---------|---------------|
| **Cursor** | AI code editor (VS Code fork) | ~$500M ARR | $20/mo | No vertical-specific coding (e.g. Solidity-only IDE) |
| **Bolt.new** | Full-stack app builder from prompts | ~$20M ARR | Subscription | No post-generation maintenance/monitoring |
| **Lovable** | No-code AI app builder | ~$17M ARR | Subscription | Same gap as Bolt — build but no operate |
| **ChatPDF / PDF.ai / AskYourPDF** | Chat with PDFs | $2.4-6M ARR combined | ~$20/mo | No domain-specific PDF analysis (legal briefs, RFPs, audit reports) |
| **Chatbase** | Custom AI chatbot for websites | ~$50K MRR (solo: Danny Postma) | $19-399/mo | No proactive outreach — only reactive chat |
| **Castmagic** | Content repurposing from audio | $30K+ MRR | $19-99/mo | No distribution layer — just transforms content |
| **Zyki** | AI LinkedIn/X comment generator | $7K MRR in 30 days | Subscription | No engagement analytics or lead scoring |
| **SiteGPT** | Website chatbot builder | ~$8K MRR | Subscription | High churn (50% month 1) — retention is the gap |
| **Numerous.ai** | AI in spreadsheets | $175K/mo | Subscription | No domain-specific spreadsheet workflows |

**Key insight**: AI wrapper median revenue after 1 year is $50K-$200K ARR for survivors. 80-95% fail. Winners differentiate on distribution, not technology.

## 2. Chrome Extensions — Underrated Distribution Channel

AI-powered Chrome extension market: $2.3B in 2025, growing 22.5% CAGR.

| Product | What it does | Revenue | Gap |
|---------|-------------|---------|-----|
| **GMass** | Gmail mass email campaigns | $130K/mo | No AI personalization layer |
| **Closet Tools** | Poshmark automation | $42K/mo at $30/mo | Platform-specific — no cross-marketplace version |
| **GoFullPage** | Full webpage screenshots | $10K/mo | No annotation/collaboration layer |
| **CSS Scan** | Inspect CSS visually | $100K+ total | No design-system extraction |
| **BlackMagic.so** | Twitter CRM/analytics | $3K/mo | X-only, no cross-platform |
| **Compose AI** | Text autocomplete | $35K/mo | No tone/brand consistency |

**Key insight**: Extensions with 10K+ users can reach $1-10K/mo. The trick is picking a platform (Gmail, LinkedIn, Shopify admin) where users already spend time.

Three trends dominated recent top launches:

1. **AI-native tools** (not "AI added") — Loki.Build (#2 PH Dec 2025), AI landing page builder competing with Webflow/Framer
2. **Messaging-first platforms** — Flux (Top Products Jan 2026), AI agents inside iMessage
3. **Consolidation plays** — Griply (Jan 2026), unified goal/habit/task tracking

**Adjacent gap**: No consolidation play for *developer workflows* specifically (CI/CD status + PR reviews + deploy monitoring + incident response in one lightweight tool).

## 5. Shopify App Ecosystem — Harsh Reality for New Entrants

- Median revenue for newly launched apps in first 3 months: **$0** (across 700 tracked launches)
- Average across those 700: $39.72 total
- Marketing category apps average $19.9K/yr — best performing category
- 0% commission on first $1M earned (since 2021)

**Verdict**: Terrible cold-start economics unless you have an existing audience or unique distribution angle. Skip unless you have a Shopify-adjacent community.

## 6. Revenue Benchmarks (Reality Check)

From SaaSRanger's 1,000+ founder survey:
- **Median micro-SaaS**: $500/mo
- **70%** never break $500/mo
- **18%** reach the $1K-$5K MRR sustainability zone
- **1-2%** exceed $50K MRR
- Conservative timeline: $1K MRR takes 12-18 months median; $5K MRR takes 2-4 years

## 7. Adjacent Gaps Worth Investigating

Based on what IS working and what these products DON'T cover:

1. **Post-build operations for AI-generated apps** — Bolt.new and Lovable build apps but don't monitor, maintain, or update them. A "Vercel for vibe-coded apps" with health checks, error tracking, and one-click fixes.

2. **Domain-specific PDF/document analysis** — ChatPDF is generic. A tool that understands RFP structure, or audit report format, or legal brief conventions could charge 5-10x.

3. **AI content distribution** (not just repurposing) — Castmagic and Opus Clip transform content but don't post, schedule, or optimize distribution. The "last mile" is manual.

4. **Cross-platform extension analytics** — BlackMagic.so does Twitter only. No tool unifies social engagement data across X + LinkedIn + GitHub activity for developer personal brands.

5. **Churn prevention for micro-SaaS** — Churnkey ($30K MRR) serves mid-market. No lightweight, $19/mo churn toolkit exists for solo founders with <500 customers (dunning emails, cancellation flows, win-back sequences).

6. **AI meeting action-item enforcement** — Fireflies/Otter/tl;dv transcribe and summarize, but nobody tracks whether action items actually get completed. A thin integration layer between meeting notes and project management.
