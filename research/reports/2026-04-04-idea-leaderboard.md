## BriefBot Score Correction (Iteration 9) -- MAJOR

BriefBot (Iter 2) was scored 18/25 with Competition = 4, based on the claim "no AI conversational intake exists." **This is no longer true.** Iteration 9 discovered **at least 5 direct/adjacent competitors** that have launched or matured:

- **Perspective AI** (getperspective.ai) -- **Near-exact match.** AI intake that replaces static forms with adaptive conversations. Qualifies, routes, summarizes leads. Targets law firms, healthcare, agencies. Deploys in <5 minutes.
- **Dashform** (getaiform.com) -- AI-native conversational form builder. $19/mo (1,000 AI conversations). Free tier. Quiz funnels + lead qualification.
- **Deformity AI** (deformity.ai) -- "Forms that talk back." Conversational AI forms. Free (100 AI responses), Pro $25/mo.
- **CloseBot** (closebot.com) -- AI appointment setter. Conversational lead qualification over SMS/chat/email. $0.012/message. Agency-first.
- **CrispForms** (crispforms.com) -- Free conversational AI form builder. Unlimited forms and submissions.
- **Formless.ai** -- Another conversational AI form competitor.

Competition score revised 4 -> 2. Market gap revised 4 -> 3. Monetization revised 4 -> 3 (price compression from free tools).
**Corrected total: 14/25.** Drops from tied #1 to below the bar. Most significant correction across all 9 iterations.
