# Tool Ideas — Iteration 10 (2026-04-11)

**Strategy**: Explore genuinely new categories not covered in iterations 1-9. Focused on compliance + computation pattern, emerging regulation tools, and underserved professional niches. 11 new categories explored.

## Categories Explored (all new)

### 1. Nutrition Facts Label Generator (FDA compliance)
- FDA 21 CFR 101.9 requires specific format. Computation: %DV, rounding rules.
- **Already free**: OnlineLabels (free), Packify (free), Nutrition-Facts-Labels.com (free/no signup), ArtworkFlow (free). Paid options: ReciPal, LabelCalc, Menusano.
- **Kill**: Pattern #1. Multiple free tools with FDA compliance. No pricing power.

### 2. AI Architectural Rendering (sketch → photorealistic)
- 23+ tools in 2026 roundups: mnml.ai, ArchiVinci, MyArchitectAI, RenderAI, Vibe3D.
- 44% of architects already using AI for concept images (Chaos State of ArchViz 2025).
- **Kill**: Pattern #3 (image tools saturated) + #7 (competition is the problem). Massively crowded.

### 3. FLSA Overtime Calculator (wage compliance)
- DOL provides FREE official FLSA Overtime Calculator Advisor at webapps.dol.gov.
- Free: ClickTime Excel template, PieceworkPro, USA Tax Calculator, over-time-calculator.com.
- **Kill**: Pattern #8 (calculators = lead gen) + #1 (government provides free tool).

### 4. Rent Increase Notice Generator (landlord compliance)
- Free tools: UtilityProfit (AI + free), LawDistrict (free PDF/Word), eForms (free), ezLandlordForms.
- Minimal computation (notice days + % cap). Mostly template filling.
- **Kill**: Pattern #1 + #10 (compliance alone without computation ≠ pricing power).

### 5. Employee Handbook Generator (HR compliance)
- 20+ tools in 2026 roundups. Free: Handbooks.io, Waybook, QuillBot, SweetProcess trial.
- Paid: AirMason, SixFifty ($250/mo), HandbookGenerator ($49 one-time).
- **Kill**: Pattern #1. Market fully served across all price points.

### 6. GDPR Website Compliance Scanner (cookie/privacy audit)
- Free: ComplyDog (no account needed), Cookiebot, 2GDPR. Enterprise: OneTrust, SecurePrivacy, Osano.
- 7+ scanners compared in 2026 roundups. PDF reports available in pro tiers.
- **Kill**: Pattern #1 + saturated. Privacy version of ScanAble's space but FAR more crowded.

### 7. DMARC/SPF/DKIM Compliance Report
- Established market: EasyDMARC, DMARCLY, dmarcian, PowerDMARC (starting $8/mo).
- Value is in ONGOING monitoring, not one-time reports. Subscription SaaS territory.
- **Kill**: Pattern #5 (subscription incumbents block pay-per-use). Not transactional.

### 8. AI Trademark Search / Conflict Checker
- Enterprise incumbents: Corsearch/TrademarkNow (172.5M records, 188 jurisdictions), Clarivate RiskMark, Trademarkia.ai, TMSearch.ai, Huski.ai.
- Data moat impossible to replicate. Free USPTO TESS exists for basics.
- **Kill**: Data moat + enterprise incumbents. Can't compete on data coverage.

### 9. Lien Waiver Generator (construction compliance)
- LienShield: free tier (3/mo), Pro $19/mo unlimited. Built.com: free generator.
- 12 states with statutory forms but computation is minimal (amounts entered, not calculated).
- **Kill**: Pattern #10 (compliance alone ≠ pricing power). Competition exists at good price points.

### 10. Data Anonymization Tool (GDPR PII removal)
- Free: Tomedes (no signup), Parabola (free CSV), terrific.tools (browser-based, data never leaves).
- Enterprise: K2view, Klippa, ARX open-source.
- **Kill**: Pattern #1. Browser-based free tools mean zero friction to get this done free.

### 11. EU AI Act Compliance Assessment (emerging regulation)
- EU Commission provides FREE official AI Act Compliance Checker.
- euaicompass.com: 25+ free interactive tools, no login required.
- Digital SME Alliance: free conformity tool. AI Act Service Desk: free checker.
- **Kill**: Pattern #1 + new pattern #14. Government/regulatory bodies now release free compliance tools proactively.

## New Kill Pattern Identified

**#14: Governments now proactively release free compliance tools.** DOL (overtime), EU Commission (AI Act), USPTO (trademark search) — regulatory bodies increasingly ship free digital tools alongside new regulations. The window between "new regulation" and "free government tool" has shrunk to near-zero. Don't assume emerging regulations = tool gap.

## Broader Conclusion

This iteration explored 11 genuinely new categories spanning food compliance, construction, HR, privacy, email security, IP law, architecture, labor law, and emerging AI regulation. **ALL killed.** Every category either has free tools, government-provided tools, or established incumbents.

Combined with iterations 1-9: **120+ tool ideas scanned across 10 iterations. ScanAble remains the ONLY idea above the 16+ threshold.** The transactional utility tool market in 2026 has achieved near-complete coverage in every category accessible to a solo developer.

## RADICAL APPROACH: Revenue-first & Arbitrage Hunting

After the standard approach killed 11 categories, I tried a fundamentally different strategy: starting from PROVEN revenue, price arbitrage, platform gaps, and Fiverr automation opportunities.

### Revenue-proven patterns analyzed:
- **HeadshotPro** ($300K/mo): "Professional service ($300) → AI tool ($29)" arbitrage. Specificity drives revenue.
- **Formula Bot** ($226K MRR): "Tedious syntax → natural language" pattern. Built with Bubble.io.
- **Virtual Staging** ($1.33B market): Now racing to bottom ($0.23-$5/image). 12+ tools. Mature.

### Arbitrage attempts (professional service → $29 tool):

### Platform gap attempts:
- Shopify pay-per-use utility apps — Market dominated by subscription apps. No clear transactional gap found.
- AI Agent builders — **KILLED**: 13+ no-code builders (Lindy, MindStudio, Gumloop). Marketplaces exist.

### Computational/optimization attempts:
- Wedding Seating Chart Optimizer — **KILLED**: Free tools (Wedibox, Zola, WeddingWire, Planning.wedding). Pattern #8 (lead gen for wedding platforms).

### SaaS-adjacent attempts:
- Changelog/Release Notes — **KILLED**: 13+ tools, all subscription ($19-249/mo). Not transactional.
- AI Product Demo Video — **KILLED**: 14+ tools (Synthesia, Trupeer, Clueso, Supademo). $20-120/mo.
- Brand Voice Checker — **KILLED**: Jasper ($49/mo), Grammarly Business, Writer, Sprites. Feature within larger platforms.

### Key insight from radical approach:
The "professional service → AI tool" arbitrage pattern was the WINNING formula of 2023-2024. By 2026, every viable arbitrage has been claimed: headshots, interior design, virtual staging, voice-over, product photos, dating photos, video production, 3D models. There is no remaining professional service that costs $200+ AND can be automated AND hasn't already been automated.
