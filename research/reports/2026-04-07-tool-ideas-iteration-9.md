# Tool Ideas — Iteration 9 (2026-04-07)

**Strategy**: Explore genuinely unexplored categories (redaction, video privacy, GEO optimization, alt text, audio processing, legal docs, stock calculators). Focus on compliance-gated + computation-required pattern that worked for ScanAble.

---

## Categories Explored (all new)

### 1. PDF/Document Redaction (PII removal)
- Compliance-gated (GDPR, HIPAA, legal). AI detects and redacts PII.
- **Already crowded**: Redactable (AI-powered, $20 for dozens of docs), SmallPDF (free redaction), Foxit Smart Redact (99.9% accuracy), Nitro Smart Redact (30+ PII types), OnlineRedactorPDF (free), PDF-Redaction.com (free).
- **Kill**: Free tools + enterprise tools bracket the space. No gap in the middle.

### 2. Video Face Blur (GDPR privacy)
- Compliance-gated (GDPR). Auto-detect and blur faces in video.
- **Already crowded**: Blur.it, BlurMe, Gallio PRO, SecureRedact, Facit AI, VideoBGRemover. 6+ dedicated tools.
- **Kill**: Saturated. Multiple tools already solve this specific problem.

### 3. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) Audit
- New category (2025-2026). Check how brand appears in AI search results.
- **Already crowded**: 22+ tools listed in category roundups. HubSpot offers FREE AEO grader. AI Rank Checker (pay-per-check), SE Ranking ($65/mo), Rankability ($149/mo), RankZero ($89/mo), Scrunch AI ($250/mo), Profound AI ($99/mo).
- **Kill**: Free + 22 competitors. Category filled within months of emerging (confirming kill pattern #14).

### 4. Bulk Alt Text Generator (WCAG compliance)
- Compliance-gated (WCAG requires alt text). AI analyzes images → generates descriptions.
- **Already crowded**: AltText.ai (85K users, 30M+ images, pay-per-credit), AltTextLab, Caseo.ai (Shopify), SmartAlt (Shopify), Describer.pro ($19/mo), AutoAlt.ai. First 25 credits free on AltText.ai.
- **Kill**: 5+ dedicated tools. Market leader has 85K users. Free tier available.

### 5. VPAT (Voluntary Product Accessibility Template) Generator
- Accessibility Tracker ($19/mo), AllAccessible (free trial). AI fills VPAT from audit data.
- **Kill**: Already exists. Natural feature of ScanAble, not standalone.

### 6. Audio Stem Separation
- LALAL.AI dominant (10 stem types), 9+ competitors. StemSplit ($0.10/min), Moises ($3.99/mo).
- **Kill**: Capital-intensive (ML models), 9+ competitors, price race.

### 7. Stock Option Tax Calculator
- ESO Fund, Carta, EquitySimplified, AgentCalc, Secfi — ALL FREE.
- **Kill**: Kill pattern #8 (calculators are lead-gen tools for financial services).

### 8. Legal Document Generators (eviction notices, C&D letters)
- Rocket Lawyer, LegalZoom, LawDepot cover broadly. AI contract/NDA killed (free).
- **Kill**: Compliance alone ≠ pricing power (pattern #10). No computation required.

## Broader Observations

This iteration explored 8+ genuinely new categories that hadn't appeared in previous iterations. **ALL mapped to existing kill patterns:**

- Document redaction → free + enterprise brackets the space
- Video privacy → 6+ dedicated tools (saturated)
- GEO/AI search tools → 22+ tools filled in <12 months (pattern #14)
- Alt text generation → 5+ tools, market leader has 85K users
- Audio processing → capital-intensive ML + 9 competitors
- Calculators → lead-gen for service companies (pattern #8)
- Legal docs → no computation = no pricing power (pattern #10)

**The compliance-gated + computation pattern remains the strongest.**  ScanAble (WCAG + axe-core computation + PDF report) is the ONLY idea across 9 tool iterations that combines all three ingredients (compliance, computation, underserved price point).

## New Kill Pattern Identified

**#13: "Compliance-adjacent" ≠ compliance-gated.** Redaction, alt text, and VPAT are adjacent to compliance but don't require the same per-output accuracy guarantee as a compliance report. Users can manually review/fix AI output. Only tools where the OUTPUT ITSELF is the compliance artifact have pricing power.
