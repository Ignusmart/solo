# B2B SaaS Unmet Demand Signals — April 2026

Raw signals from Reddit, HN, and aggregator sites. Filtered for B2B, non-Web3-security, non-biotech, non-LATAM-specific.

---

## Strongest Signals (validated demand + pricing gap)

**1. Google Review Automation for SMBs** — BirdEye/Podium charge $349-399/mo. 36M+ US small businesses need affordable review request tools. Massive pricing gap at $29-49/mo tier. Competitors moved upmarket.

**2. Failed Payment Recovery for Indie SaaS** — Stripe recovers only ~23% of failed payments by default. Churnkey charges $250/mo, unaffordable for small SaaS. Involuntary churn costs founders 5-18% MRR. Clear $19-49/mo gap.

**3. Lightweight CRM for Solo Freelancers** — HoneyBook raised prices 89%. Freelance CRM market validated at $140M ARR. Solo operators priced out as tools chase agencies. Gap at $9-19/mo.

**4. Feature Voting / Public Roadmap at Indie Scale** — Canny starts $79/mo, UserVoice even higher. Indie SaaS founders need this at $15-39/mo. Validated pain across r/SaaS and Indie Hackers.

**5. EU Compliance Docs Generator (GDPR, AI Act, NIS2)** — Small SaaS increasingly blocked from enterprise deals without compliance docs. No affordable automated solution. Consultants charge thousands. High willingness to pay.

## Strong Signals (clear pain, less pricing data)

**6. Booking Software for Service Trades** — Tattoo studios, dog groomers, auto detailers need vertical-specific workflows (deposits, waivers, before/after photos). Calendly too generic. Lower churn in vertical solutions.

**7. White-Label Client Reporting for Freelancer Agencies** — Freelancers managing 5-20 local clients spend hours monthly copying metrics into reports. AgencyAnalytics/DashThis target larger shops only.

**8. Vendor Expense / SaaS Subscription Analyzer** — Businesses waste money on unused subscriptions. No simple tool for small teams (<50 people). Enterprise tools (Zylo, Productiv) start at $1K+/mo.

**9. Automated SOP Generator for Agencies** — Agency SOPs become outdated fast, knowledge is lost when people leave. No lightweight tool exists; everyone uses Notion docs that rot.

**10. Inventory Alert Tool for Small Shopify Stores** — No alerts for slow-moving inventory or reorder points. Shopify analytics show what sold, not what to worry about. Cash flow impact is real.

## HN Developer Tool Wishes (Dec 2025 thread)

## Key Pricing Insight

The recurring pattern: incumbents moved upmarket, leaving a $15-49/mo gap for solo operators, freelancers, and micro-businesses. The biggest opportunity is offering 80% of what enterprise tools do at 10% of the price.
