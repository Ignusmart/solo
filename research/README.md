# Research & Reports

Pre-build research, market analysis, and strategic planning. Do the thinking here before writing code.

## Structure

- **`strategy/`** — Long-lived strategy documents (income ideas, playbooks, skills profile). Numbered for reading order.
- **`reports/`** — Point-in-time analyses, competitive research, feasibility studies. Date-prefixed for chronological ordering.

## Adding New Research

### Reports (one-off analyses)
Name as `YYYY-MM-DD-topic.md`. Examples:
- `2026-04-05-mcp-market-landscape.md`
- `2026-04-10-plata-competitor-teardown.md`

### Strategy (living docs)
Add to the numbered series or create a new numbered file:
- `06-PROGRESS-LOG.md`
- `07-DISTRIBUTION-STRATEGY.md`

## Report Template

```markdown
# [Title] — [Month Year]

## Context
Why this research matters right now.

## Key Findings
- Finding 1
- Finding 2

## Recommendation
What to do based on this research.

## Sources
- [Source 1](url)
```
